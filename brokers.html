Broker Program

Two tiers designed around performance and trust.

Every relationship starts in our Broker Associate tier. Demonstrate consistency, accuracy, and borrower care, and we’ll invite you into the Core Partner circle.

  1. Broker Associates

    We cultivate relationships with qualified professionals who consistently bring us high-quality, well-packaged leads. Broker Associates earn 0.25 points per closed loan, paid at closing.

    Future Core Broker Partners are drawn from this group—consistency, accuracy, and professionalism are the path to promotion.

  2. Core Broker Partners

    • 0.50 points per closed loan, paid at closing.
    • $100 immediate payout for each submitted lead that perfectly matches our Ideal Deal Box—before the loan closes.
    • Relationship continuity compensation: if a borrower they introduced returns for future financing, the Core Broker continues to earn 0.25 points on each subsequent loan.

    We believe the person who brought us the relationship should continue to share in its success.
