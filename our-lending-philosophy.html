Guiding principles

Discipline, transparency, and partnership drive every loan.

Here are the ten commitments we bring to every borrower relationship.

  1. We lend for builders, not just projects

    Every loan is the start of a relationship. We want borrowers who plan to grow—and we reward those who execute with discipline and transparency.

  2. Performance earns better terms

    We believe risk is dynamic. As milestones are hit and uncertainty falls, your rate can step down. Progress deserves recognition, not penalty.

  3. Speed shouldn’t mean chaos

    Fast capital should still be thoughtful. Our tech stack streamlines underwriting and draws, so you move quickly—without sacrificing clarity.

  4. Transparency is non-negotiable

    No hidden fees, no surprise compounding, no vague fine print. You’ll know every cost up front, in plain language.

  5. Capital should be predictable, not punitive

    We design structures that create breathing room—fixed-rate simplicity, clear extension options, and draw schedules that keep your project moving instead of trapping your cash flow.

  6. Flexibility for the real world

    We understand weather, permits, and supply chains don’t always cooperate. Our grace-period and extension programs are built around reason, not rigidity.

  7. Technology should simplify, not complicate

    From digital inspections to same-day draw approvals, we invest in tools that save you time and paperwork—not ones that get in your way.

  8. Compliance is a feature, not a friction

    We run a transparent, fully documented platform. Institutional discipline is part of what protects both our borrowers and investors.

  9. We price risk, not relationships

    Our underwriting is consistent and data-driven—but we treat strong operator history and proven execution as the most valuable collateral you have.

  10. We build long-term lending partnerships

    Felspar’s goal is to fund you again and again—across markets, asset types, and cycles. When you perform, your capital should get cheaper and faster over time.
