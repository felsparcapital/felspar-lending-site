Investing with Felspar

Structured. Transparent. Aligned.

At Felspar, we don’t believe in “hard money” as a transaction. We believe in structured capital—disciplined lending that’s fast, data-driven, and rooted in trust.

We lend to builders, not speculators

We finance experienced operators who improve real assets and manage risk with discipline—not speculation.

Alignment before yield

We co-invest in every deal and earn primarily from performance, not AUM growth. Your capital isn’t feeding a fee machine—it’s working in real projects alongside ours.

Data-driven risk management

Every loan passes standardized underwriting: verified collateral, ARV validation, digital inspection, and active draw oversight. Technology lets us identify and mitigate risk in real time.

Technology as a fiduciary tool

Digital inspection workflows, automated compliance tracking, and real-time dashboards are built to protect principal and keep information flowing.

Integrity compounds

We communicate clearly, decline deals that don’t fit, and never hide behind jargon. In lending, clarity is the highest form of respect.

Capital that builds real things

Every Felspar loan helps restore, improve, or create tangible property. Your returns come from productive, visible work—not abstraction.

Felspar Partners LLC works with accredited and sophisticated investors seeking participation in real-estate-backed credit opportunities. To request access to current offerings, please complete our investor qualification form. Deal information is provided only after accreditation review.
